EXPLORE WHAT'S INSIDE THE COURSE

Get to know the course

Get hands-on experience with Google Analytics 4, Tag Manager, and Looker Studio in an 8-week live course designed for marketers and analysts.

How long is the course?

The course will last for 8 weeks. Each week, we will host 2 live sessions lasting 2 hours each. All classes are virutal.

Where will the course take place?

The live sessions will be hosted on Zoom. Before every lesson, you will receive a link to join the class.

What will each lesson include?

Lessons will include live walkthroughs, hands-on practice, weekly homework, practice labs, and interactive quizzes.

Reserve your spot
What will you learn in the "Stategize" section?

You will learn how to transform key business questions and metrics into a dynamic framework known as a "Measurement Plan," composed of events, event parameters, and metrics. Additionally, you will master the use of these components to confidently address critical business questions with data-driven insights.

What will you learn in the "Implement" sections?

You will gain hands-on experience using Google Tag Manager and gtag.js to implement tracking for Google Analytics 4, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other platforms. Additionally, you will learn to write custom code for tracking forms and other critical user actions. By the end of the course, you’ll also be introduced to advanced analytics platforms such as Segment, Amplitude, and Mixpanel, broadening your analytics toolkit.

What will you learn in the "Visualize" section?

You will learn to visualize data from Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, and other sources to create impactful reports and dashboards that empower stakeholders to make data-driven decisions based on your insights. Along the way, you’ll be introduced to data visualization best practices, equipping you with the skills to effectively represent diverse datasets. By the end of the course, you will also gain exposure to visualizing data from BigQuery, further expanding your expertise.
